DSMB Guided Training Prototype

How to open this on your computer

1. Download the zip file.
2. Unzip or extract the folder. Do not open index.html from inside the compressed zip preview.
3. Open the extracted folder named dsmb-training-prototype.
4. Double-click index.html.
5. If prompted, open it with Chrome, Edge, Safari, or Firefox.

Important

- Keep the files and folders together. The page needs app.js, styles.css, data, and assets to stay beside index.html.
- This is a standalone training prototype. It does not require CROMS login or internet access.
- The names, study identifiers, grant numbers, document titles, emails, and dates are synthetic/deidentified training data.

If the page opens blank or your browser blocks local files

Mac:

1. Open Terminal.
2. Run:

   cd "PATH_TO_THE_EXTRACTED/dsmb-training-prototype"
   python3 -m http.server 8080

3. Open this URL in a browser:

   http://127.0.0.1:8080/

Windows, if Python is installed:

1. Open Command Prompt or PowerShell.
2. Run:

   cd "PATH_TO_THE_EXTRACTED\dsmb-training-prototype"
   py -m http.server 8080

3. Open this URL in a browser:

   http://127.0.0.1:8080/
